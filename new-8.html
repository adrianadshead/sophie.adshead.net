Description of Project:


The assignment I chose to add here is the HTML coding assignment. This may be my second favorite assignment we did all semester (my favorite being this whole online portfolio assignment, I get to do so many cool things with this assignment). For this assignment, we were told to create a fan page for a movie/tv/book series that we like. I chose to make a fan page about my all time favorite book series, Percy Jackson and the Heroes of Olympus. My website includes four internal pages and several external links to other websites. It includes a page each about the three main characters of the series, and the main antagonist.
